Short Game Golf

A fun and simple VR golf game designed to help you work on that short game

David Ferris

CPS643 Virtual Reality - Toronto Metropolitan University, Department of Computer Science

Description

Short Game Golf is a fun, immersive, and interactive VR golf game developed using unity for the Meta Quest 2. Short Game Golf is a modified mini-golf experience with slightly larger courses which allows players to utilize a chipper or a putter club to work on their stroke, overcome obstacles, collect pickups, and complete courses in as few hits as possible!

Hills, sand, and obstacles challenge the player to select the best club to overcome the various situations they find themselves in throughout the course with each club having its own strengths and weaknesses. The chipper is optimal for clearing large distances and maneuvering over obstacles while the putter provides the best ground game accuracy and speed.

Players utilize natural interactions and diegetic interfaces to manipulate clubs, take shots, check their scores, and move to take their next shot. In combination with the use of spatial audio and haptic feedback Short Game Golf creates an immersive and fun player experience.
